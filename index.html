Top three activities or places in Kolkata

Visit Howrah Bridge

Howrah Bridge, a sizable steel bridge built over the Hooghly River, is a well-known landmark of Kolkata.

Zoo in Kolkata

The earliest zoological park to have been founded in India is the Alipore Zoo, also known as the Calcutta Zoo or the Zoological Garden of Alipore.

Durga Puja Festival

Attending the Durga Puja event in Kolkata is a must if you want to really experience the culture and life of Kolkata.
